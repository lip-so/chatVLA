How Plug & Play Works

Cloud-Powered Robotics

Plug & Play runs LeRobot entirely in the cloud on Tune Robotics servers. You don't need to install anything locally - just connect your robots and start working.

Port Detection

Our system detects USB ports where your robots are connected and provides intelligent suggestions for robot types based on the detected hardware.

Robot Calibration

Calibrate your robot arms to ensure proper joint positioning and movement ranges. This establishes the baseline for accurate control and teleoperation.

Real-time Teleoperation

Control your robot in real-time using a leader device. Set up both leader and follower robots for intuitive manual control with optional camera feedback.

Dataset Recording

Record robot demonstrations to create training datasets. Configure recording parameters and automatically push datasets to Hugging Face Hub for sharing.

Supported Robots

  • SO101: 6-DOF robotic arm with gripper
  • SO100: Compact robotic arm for desktop use
  • Koch: High-precision research arm
